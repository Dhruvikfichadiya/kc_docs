Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 4, 2024

Welcome to KissanConnect! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”), designed to provide farmers with direct market access to sell their crops, fruits, and other agricultural products. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, contact details (email address, phone number), and any other information you provide when registering for an account or interacting with the App.
  • Business Information: Details related to your agricultural produce, including crop types, quantities, pricing, and any other relevant data that you provide to list your products.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and usage patterns.
  • Device Information: Details about your device, such as its model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and Improve: Operate, maintain, and enhance the App, enabling you to list and sell your agricultural products and facilitating transactions.
  • Personalize: Customize the App experience according to your preferences and needs as a farmer or buyer.
  • Communicate: Send you updates, notifications, and other information related to the App, including customer support and promotional messages.
  • Analyze: Understand and analyze usage patterns to improve the App and better serve your needs.
  • Legal Obligations: Comply with legal requirements and protect our rights and interests.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App and providing services, such as payment processing and analytics.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or in response to legal processes or requests from public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access and Update: Access and update your personal and business information by contacting us through the App or via email.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of receiving promotional communications by following the instructions provided in those communications or contacting us.
